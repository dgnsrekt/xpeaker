Xpeaker

Read your timeline out loud.

A Chrome extension that reads X / Twitter posts aloud, right in your timeline. Click the speaker on any post, or drive it from the keyboard. Everything runs on your device — no account, no servers, no tracking.

  • ✓ Single or continuous "thread" reading that auto-scrolls
  • ✓ Per-author voices, or a distinct voice auto-assigned per author
  • ✓ Karaoke word-highlight captions as it reads
  • ✓ Reads full "Show more" posts; skips promoted/ad posts
  • ✓ Keyboard shortcuts — Default or Vim-ish
View on GitHub Privacy policy

High-quality neural voices come from the free "Supertonic Text-to-Speech Voices" companion extension; without it, Xpeaker uses your browser's built-in voices.
